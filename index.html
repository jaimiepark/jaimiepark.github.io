About

I'm a Postdoc Researcher at Data Science Lab in School of Computing, KAIST, advised by Meeyoung (Mia) Cha. My research lies at the intersection of social computing, human factors in computing, and online advertising. I am interested in exploring how people search, acquire, perceive, and share information on social media. I am especially interested in understanding the impact of online social networks on consumers' purchasing decisions.
